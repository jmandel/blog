This architecture is the forcing function for recognizing (explicitly or tacitly) AI agents as drivers of interoperability. Agents will increasingly be tools like a mouse and keyboard -- i.e., the interfaces by which users interact with apps. Locking them out won't be the answer. Claiming every pixel is a licensed "interoperability element" is also a non answer.
