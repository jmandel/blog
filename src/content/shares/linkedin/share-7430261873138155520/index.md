SMART Health IT team submitted formal comments on HTI-5. Feel free to read and borrow language. Briefly: Deregulation should not mean losing sight of whether interoperability is actually working. (HTI-5 risks eliminating the instrumentation ASTP/ONC needs to steer a successful transition to FHIR-based exchange.)

Three priorities: 1) ensure AI agents cannot be priced or contracted out of access, 2) preserve Real World Testing for full EHI export, and 3)  retain meaningful Bulk FHIR observability metrics.
